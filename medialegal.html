Media Policy

We are happy to let you place videos of any of our games on video sites such as YouTube. We also have no issues with you monetising those videos using the YouTube partner program or similar program on other video sharing sites.

The same is applied to screenshots, or even edits of screenshots. If you wish to place those screenshots onto blogs or websites, we have no issues.

The way we see it, is that every video blogger, or YouTuber has to make money for promoting games, even if that promotion is a negative one. Playing, recording and editing videos takes time and should be treated like a job (we view it as a job!). This is the way that promotion works in many other mediums, and to us, and we will happily continue with this mindset for the foreseeable future.
