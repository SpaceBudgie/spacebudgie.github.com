No Way Out

Description


Make your way through an endless series of rooms, taking on increasing numbers of enemy robots and drones, in an attempt to make big bucks!

- Online coop multiplayer
- 40 levels, which scale in difficulty as you progress
- 2 weapons, 2 enemy types
- Disco lights!

Controls:
Movement: W,A,S,D Mouse
FireGun: Left Click
GunSelect: Q
Tab: Chat/Menu

You can read more on the development and final thoughts of No Way Out on Gamasutra here: 7dfps Challenge: Smash TV Inspired FPS (No Way Out!)
